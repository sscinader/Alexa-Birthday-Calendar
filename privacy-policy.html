This Privacy Policy is for the Alexa Skill: "Birthdays".

The Birthday Skill is non-commercial, with freely available source code at https://github.com/sscinader/Alexa-Birthday-Calendar.

The Birthday Skill does maintain names and birthdays that are entered into it. The names and birthdays are stored in Amazon Web Service.

There will be no use for any commercial or other purpose of this information by the maintainers of this skill.

This Skill was created by Sadie Cinader and her dad, Arthur Cinader Jr. as an educational exercise in programming and interface design.
